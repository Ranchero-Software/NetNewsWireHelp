## What’s Happening Right Now

This lists activities that do something over the web: downloading feeds, syncing statuses, finding and downloading favicons, importing OPML, finding and adding feeds, and so on.

It can be useful to know what the app is doing right now. Is it syncing with iCloud? Is it downloading a bunch of feeds? Talking to your FreshRSS installation? Downloading feed images? This window has those answers.

<div class=info>
Most activities happen in just a fraction of a second, too fast to read — so there’s an extra couple seconds of linger time for each activity in this window. In other words: things are happening faster than it appears!
</div>

## Also Useful

The [Activity Log](activity-log.html) lists the most recent activity — instead of showing only what’s happening right now, it lists what *did* happen and with additional detail.

The [Error Log](error-log.html) lists recent errors, also with additional detail.

The two buttons at the bottom of the screen open these logs.
